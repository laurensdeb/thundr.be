What’s up with our Federal Parliament? A look at the Chamber of Representatives through its data.

7 May 2021·6 mins
The last few weeks we’ve been working on a project for the Datavisualisation course of Bart Mesuere along with four fellow students. Over the course of 12 weeks we’ve developed a Scraper/API and built a number of very interesting visualizations on the Chamber of Representatives of our Federal Parliament. In this post we’ll go into detail about the data and story behind this project.
