Curriculum vitae

Laurens Debackere is a Computer Science student at Ghent University. In 2021 he started offering IT consultancy services for businesses interested in using Solid and Linked Data to optimize their operations and improve user experience.

Laurens is a Software Developer with experience in building microservices following the DevOps principles, using modern cloud-native technologies like Docker, Kubernetes and Spring Boot. While obtaining his Bachelor in Computer Science he first learned about the Solid Project, and has since gathered experience with the technology both in server-side contexts as well as in client-side applications. In the context of his thesis he is researching ways to adapt the Solid specification to enterprise settings, while retaining the original principles of data governance and interoperability at the heart of the project.
